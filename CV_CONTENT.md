## Professional Experience

### Research Fellow in Data Engineering
**Peace and Conflict Resolution Evidence Programme (PeaceRep), University of Edinburgh**  
*November 2021 – Present*

- Co-creator and lead developer of the PA-X Tracker, an interactive platform for exploring peace and transition process data
- Co-designer of the PA-X Tracker Globe, a 3D visualization integrating peace agreement and conflict data
- Lead data engineer for the Perceptions of Peace in South Sudan survey programme (2021–2025), managing 22,677 respondents across six waves
- Co-creator of the Peace Agreement Actors Dataset (PAA-X), enabling network analysis across peace agreements
- Developer of data pipelines, NLP workflows, and analytical tools for peace and conflict research

**Co-creator with Sanja Badanjak**  
*2025*

### Perceptions of Peace in South Sudan Surveys
**Principal Data Engineer**  
*2021–2025*  
Harvard Dataverse, DOI: 10.7910/DVN/E5VXV4

Six-wave longitudinal survey programme (2021–2025) covering 22,677 respondents across 29 locations in South Sudan. Data cleaned, curated, and prepared for public access and reuse by researchers and practitioners.
